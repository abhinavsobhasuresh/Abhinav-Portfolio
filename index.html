About Me

With a strong passion for Hardware Engineering and Embedded Systems, I work on developing practical and innovative electronics solutions. I focus on combining theoretical knowledge with real-world implementation to create reliable circuit designs and product-focused hardware.

Classic Portfolio Design

A 4-layer PCB designed for an ESP32-based system using EasyEDA. The board incorporates dedicated power and ground planes to enhance signal integrity, reduce noise, and ensure stable power distribution. The multilayer stack-up supports high-speed signal routing, improved EMI performance, and compact layout optimization, making it suitable for reliable embedded and IoT applications.
